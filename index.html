Harada Method Template

Transform your goal into 64 actionable steps

How to Use This Template

  1. Write your main goal in the center yellow cell
  2. Identify 8 key focus areas in the gray cells surrounding the center
  3. For each focus area, define 8 specific action steps in the white cells
  4. Work through your 64 action steps systematically to achieve your goal
Inspired by Takashi Harada
